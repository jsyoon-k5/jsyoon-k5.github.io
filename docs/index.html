June-Seop Yoon

I am a Ph.D. student at Yonsei University's Esports & High Performance HCI Lab, advised by Prof. Byungjoo Lee. My research focuses on computational models of Esports players and their applications in the Esports industry, such as player training, evaluation, and performance analysis. Specifically, I am interested in understanding what enables professional players to outperform others.
