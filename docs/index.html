June-Seop Yoon

I am a Ph.D. student at Yonsei Esports & High Performance HCI Lab, advised by Prof. Byungjoo Lee. My research focuses on computational models of Esports players and their applications in the Esports industry, such as player training, performance evaluation, and performance analysis. Specifically, I aim to understand what enables professional FPS players to achieve outstanding performance through computational models. I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Yonsei University.
